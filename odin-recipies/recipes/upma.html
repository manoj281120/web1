Upma

Ingredients:

  • Semolina
  • Spices
  • Lentils
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Use a non-stick or heavy bottom pan to prevent sticking and burning.
  2. Make sure onions are soft and shiny before adding the next ingredient.
  3. You can add all the ingredients to the pot at once and cook them, then add water and bring it to a boil before adding the semolina.
  4. You can also add all the ingredients to the pot, including the semolina, but boil the water separately and add it to the ingredients.
